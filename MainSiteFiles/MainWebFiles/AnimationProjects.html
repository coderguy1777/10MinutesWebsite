The Robot

The Robot is an animated skit, meant to demonstrate abilities of texturing
within the UV Texturing software known as substance painter, and using materials created
from Algorithmic substance designer. The story behind the skit is quite simple due to
the purpose of it, that mainly being to demonstrate simple skills. But mainly, the story
is about a Robot, who happens to wake up, at the end of a great war between an unknown alien
race, and only lives long enough just to see the end of the war, before running out of power
and dieing, watching the sunset, and the war coming to an end.
